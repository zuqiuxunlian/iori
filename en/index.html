IORI Timer

Football training stopwatch | Match timing | Camera Shutter Remote Control

Small change, Big difference →

Simple operation

Press and hold ready, release to start the timer, press again to stop the timer, Press hold again to re-ready the timer. Use it once and you won't forget it.

Remote Control

After connecting the mobile phone through Bluetooth, the button synchronously controls the start and stop of the mobile phone camera. You can easy send the video to the coach to help analyze.

Children's friendly

The large touchable buttons allow for self-testing without coach assistance, creating a competitive training culture and improving athletic performance.
